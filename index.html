What is the time complexity of Dijkstra's algorithm and why?
Clarity AI · Step-by-step:

Dijkstra's Algorithm Complexity

Step 1 — Setup: We maintain a priority queue (min-heap) of vertices. Initially all distances = infinity except source = 0.

Step 2 — Per vertex: Each vertex is extracted from the heap once — O(V log V) total extractions.

Step 3 — Per edge: Each edge is relaxed at most once — O(E log V) for heap updates.

Result: O((V + E) log V) with a binary min-heap. For dense graphs (E ≈ V²), this becomes O(V² log V).
